About This Tool

The Mobile Legends Rank Calculator helps players estimate how many matches they need to reach a higher rank based on their current rank and win rate. This tool is designed to help players plan their ranked grind more efficiently.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator compares your current rank and target rank, then uses your win rate to estimate the total matches required. The result is an approximation and actual progress may vary depending on gameplay and matchmaking.
